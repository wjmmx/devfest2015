COACH CLINIC

  • Is your agile adoption going smoothly?
  • Did you ever encounter anything uncomfortable or any difficulties?
  • Do you want to talk to experts to see what your problems are? If you feel your agile adoption ok, what can you do next?
  • If you want to get started, are you expecting advice to avoid pitfalls?

    • As participants, you will be able to talk to experts face to face. They will clarify your confusions, share experience and knowledges.
    Coach Clinic Process
    Signing up
  • Time box at 30 mins, if there is no client in queue, you can extend another time box
  • Time for Coach Clinic

    2. Oct. 21st, 13:00 - 15:00
    3. Oct. 21st, 15:30 - 16:30

  • Coach clinic will be closed during lunch time and tea break, while you’re encouraged to have a lunch appointment with any coach. Coach reserves the right to reject though.
  • You can sign up for multiple time boxes, but not in a continuous row
  • You can walk in and get immediate appointments if a coach is available.

    • Coaching
  • Attendees can request an appointment by filling out a sticky note and placing it on their desired time box on the sign-up board. We encourage you to post early so facilitators and coaches could prepare and coordinate. Each coach's expertise domain will be published on the sign-up board for your reference.
  • The sticky note has the person’s name (first is enough) and topic on it.
  • Attendees should show up on time at clinic.
  • Feedbacks are welcomed before exiting clinic. Feedback means will be provided onsite.
