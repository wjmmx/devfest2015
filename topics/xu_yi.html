需求条目化



[讲师介绍]

徐毅，是国内目前最资深的敏捷教练之一，他在软件测试，测试自动化，Scrum，敏捷开发，团队教练和引导等领域都具有丰富的经验。他拥有超过十年在通讯和IT领域工作的经验，拥有CSM，CSP，ITILv3等认证。
[主题内容]
Comes from the experience of 1+ year coaching for a top4 commercial bank at China. As China banks trying hard to tackle the challenges from internet companies on the 2C businesses, like Alipay or WeChat Pay. They need to enhance their strength in the market, while they found hard to catch up with the releasing speed and frequency of those internet companies. Besides the mainframe-heavy infrastructures and the long communication chain via highly functionalized organizational structure, their current model on requirements and the concept on product seem to be one of the causes as well, as it will be too costy while working with highly fragmented small software feature requirements.
  • executives have an timely vision
  • business people complain IT is too slow in responding to market
  • R&D people complain business pushed too many contents to deliver
  • product people are too few to translate between business and R&D
  • focus on user experience is not sufficient
  • managers worried about the skills redundancy and attritions
  • code design complexity continue to increase in Plaster Patching style development
    • The Requirement Itemization practice aims to reduce requirement quantity and enhance quality from the procedure aspect: shorten the communication chain, split big meeting with all different roles into smaller sequential meeting with various combination of roles, emphasize collaborative discussion around visual representation, etc.
