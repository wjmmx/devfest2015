When Scrum meet ToastMaster





[主题内容]

1. What things drive a self-organizing team work better?
2. How to communicate more smoothly within team through change roles?
3. How to host meetings more effective without delay?
4. How to be a good non-authorized servant leader?
5. What drive individuals to grow continuously?

7. ToastMaster members can't earn money from a club, why they still have great passions in it? Can our Scrum team has such culture?

[讲师介绍]

I am Yating Yang from Cisco CRDC at Shanghai, I mainly focus on testing Video end-to-end solution. I am a big fan of Agile. Agile make me love with it and in the meantime also hate it. Why?
I love Agile. I do see agile gives our engineers more space to grow. Agile requires an engineer with capabilities beyond technology, such as self-organizing, much more communication, planning, etc. For traditional software tester, if we don't change, we can't live any more. I am interested in tester transformation in Agile world.
I hate Agile. Because I see too many scrum teams are not running very well, we wear agile outfit but don't practice agile key value. I have strong interests on Agile team building, so I submit my Pecha Kucha sharing and hope I want to communicate more with Agile talents in this great event.
Personally, I love public speaking and sharing and have lots of experience on it. My long term goal is to grow to be an Agile Coach, I really hope I can get this opportunity to move one big step.
