What are Scrum Gathering

At Scrum Gathering, you'll meet like-minded Scrum practitioners, trainers, coaches, and enthusiasts. You'll also hear amazing keynote speakers, participate in open space sessions, and deepen your Scrum knowledge.

Scrum Gathering China 2016 will be held at Hangzhou city during October 21-22, 2016.

ADELE DIAMOND, the professor of developmental cognitive neuroscience at the University of British Columbia, grabs the headlines and brings to the forefront innovative ways of developing effective learning environments and strategies, looks at the world of play and being in learning in terms of cognitive science, and brings up "Learning, Doing, Being". In China, a famous scholar, writer and poem, Wang Guowei also brings the similar opinion of the 3 stages in learning.

We hope we could approach different topics via the different learning stages to have a success Gathering!
