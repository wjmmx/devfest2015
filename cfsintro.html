Scrum Gathering China 2016
Innovative Formats and Lecturer Treatment

The submission can be in any format from speech, workshop, poster or Pecha Kucha. Different formats’ objective, key elements in the submissions, preparing and review efforts vary, so treatments for accepted submissions vary. Please pay attention to details below.

SPEECH

Duration: 40 minutes sharing + 10 minutes Q&A

Lecturer Treatment (please be noted that if one submission contains two or more lecturer names, only the first one deserves the treatment):

Free access to all activities of the conference;

Participate to lecturer gathering before or after the conference;

2000 RMB or foreign currency with equivalent value compensation for lecturers from outside of Hangzhou on their transition;

3 or less nights hotel lodging for lecturers from outside of Hangzhou;

WORKSHOP

Workshop was meaning a gathering of handicraftsmen or manufacturers in a compact space to do skill study and sharing. Later on it was extended to mean a group of experts gathering to discuss and agree on some specific domain problems. Workshop is a learning pattern with experience, immersion and interaction. It has better interactivity compares with traditional speech, and can provide connections for lecturer and audience in a cross domain and cross subject way. We expect to see submission in workshop format especially in visualized facilitation, coach techniques, mobi programming, practical skills express learning, etc.

Lecturer Treatment (please be noted that if one submission contains two or more lecturer names, only the first one deserves the treatment):

Free access to all activities of the conference;

Participate to lecturer gathering before or after the conference;

2000 RMB or foreign currency with equivalent value compensation for lecturers from outside of Hangzhou on their transition;

3 or less nights hotel lodging for lecturers from outside of Hangzhou;

POSTER

The set up of poster format, is to motivate a wider and warmer discussion from the whole agile community, about most recent exploration, experience and challenges,advanced technology and disruptive ideas/ innovations, regarding agile implementation and best engineering practices. The submission’s content can be reflection about past experiences and challenges, description about on-going innovation and exercises, or forecast regarding future changes.

Lecturer Treatment (please be noted that if one submission contains two or more lecturer names, only the first one deserves the treatment):

Accepted submissions will be published on official website and onsite when the conference happens;

500 RMB coupon upon early-bird ticket price;

Participate to lecturer gathering before or after the conference;

Pecha Kucha

Pecha Kucha was originated from Japan and it is a publish speaking approach chartered with fast pace and visualization. The speaker needs to show 20 or more pictures within 6 minutes and 40 seconds with each picture having 20 seconds to elaborate. This form can avoid boring meeting and keep the content compact. It should be sparkles about agile practices and it can be anything related with agile and scrum. We expect to receive compact but complete (with 5 W answered), and logically neat submission.

Lecturer Treatment (please be noted that if one submission contains two or more lecturer names, only the first one deserves the treatment):

500 RMB coupon upon early-bird ticket price;

Participate to lecturer gathering before or after the conference;

Participate communication with Pecha Kucha fans;
